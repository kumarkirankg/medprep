ClearTriage
Clinical AI pre-processing · medication intelligence layer
🟢 Concept prototype
Clinics and telehealth platforms handle thousands of inbound patient calls daily. Medication queries — missed doses, side effects, running out of prescriptions — consume significant clinical staff time and create inconsistent triage. ClearTriage sits between the patient call and the clinical team. It analyses the call transcript and returns structured clinical intelligence. No clinical advice is given to the patient. Purely structured pre-processing.
